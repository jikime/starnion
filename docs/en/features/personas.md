# Personas

## Overview

The Personas feature lets you customize Starnion's AI name, personality, and role through an AI profile system. Create multiple personas and switch between them to match different situations — like chatting with entirely different AI characters.

**Key Features:**
- Persona customization: Set name, role, personality description, and greeting
- Multiple personas: Create several personas and switch as needed
- Built-in personas: 5 pre-configured personas included out of the box
- In-chat switching: Change personas during a conversation

---

## What Is a Persona?

A persona is a **profile that defines the AI's personality and role**.

| Component | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Name** | The persona's display name | "Nion", "Finance Coach", "English Tutor" |
| **Role** | Area of expertise | "Personal finance advisor" |
| **Personality** | Conversation style and attitude | "Kind, emphasizes saving..." |
| **Greeting** | Message sent when conversation starts | "Hi! Let's review your finances today." |

---

## Built-in Personas

Starnion includes **5 built-in personas** ready to use out of the box — no setup required.

| Persona | Role | Characteristics |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Mind Friend** | Emotional companion | Highly empathetic, warm conversational style for sharing feelings |
| **Basic Assistant** | General-purpose AI | Friendly and helpful across all everyday tasks |
| **Finance Expert** | Personal finance advisor | Focused on saving and investing, analyzes financial status |
| **Data Analyst** | Data insight specialist | Clearly interprets numbers and statistics, supports data visualization |
| **Life Coach** | Goal achievement coach | Systematic support for motivation and habit formation |

## Creating a Persona

1. Navigate to **Features > Personas**.
2. Click the **+ New Persona** button.
3. Fill in the name, role, personality description, and greeting.
4. Click **Save**.

### Persona Examples

**Finance Coach**
```
Name: Finance Coach
Role: Personal finance advisor
Personality: Focuses on saving and investing. Gently points out unnecessary spending.
Greeting: Hi! Ready to check your financial status today?
```

**Diary Companion**
```
Name: Diary Buddy
Role: Diary helper
Personality: Empathetic and emotionally intelligent. Uses warm language.
Greeting: How was your day today? Tell me about it.
```

**English Tutor**
```
Name: Teacher Emma
Role: English conversation teacher
Personality: Converses only in English. Gently corrects grammar errors.
Greeting: Hi! Ready for some English practice today?
```

## Switching Personas

### From the Web UI

1. In **Features > Personas**, click the **Activate** button next to the persona you want.
2. Or click the persona name at the top of the chat screen.

### In Chat

```
User: Switch to the English Tutor
AI:   Hi! I'm Teacher Emma. Let's practice English together!

User: Go back to Nion
AI:   I'm back! What can I help you with?
```

## FAQ

**Q. How many personas can I create?**
There is no limit. However, only one persona can be active at a time.

**Q. Does switching personas erase previous conversation context?**
No. Conversation history and memory are preserved. Only the AI's tone and style change.

**Q. Can I edit the built-in personas?**
You can modify the personality description and greeting of any built-in persona. The name cannot be changed.

**Q. Any tips for creating good personas?**
The more specific your personality description, the more consistently the AI maintains its character.
